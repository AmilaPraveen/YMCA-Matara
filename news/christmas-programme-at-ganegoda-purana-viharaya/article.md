# Christmas Programme at Ganegoda Purana Viharaya

On December 19, 2025, a Christmas carol programme was held at the Ganegoda Purana Viharaya between 4:00 PM and 6:00 PM. The event was organized to promote religious harmony among various communities.

The programme was attended by religious leaders of other faiths, including Christian and Catholic priests. Local residents from the area also participated in the event, which was held at the temple to foster unity and understanding across different religious groups.
