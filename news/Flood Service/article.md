## Standing Together in Times of Crisis

In response to the severe flooding caused by the overflowing Nilwala River this June, **YMCA Matara** mobilized an emergency relief mission to assist displaced families in the **Malimboda** and **Thihagoda** areas. Our volunteers worked tirelessly to ensure that those in temporary shelters received basic necessities.

### Relief Operations Summary
* **Emergency Meals:** Distributed over 500 cooked lunch packets to families stranded by rising water levels.
* **Dry Ration Packs:** Provided 120 families with essential kits containing rice, dhal, sugar, tea leaves, and milk powder.
* **Safe Water:** Delivered 200 liters of bottled drinking water to areas where local wells were contaminated.
* **Cleaning Assistance:** Once water levels receded, our Youth Wing assisted in cleaning mud and debris from three local primary schools to help children return to classes.
