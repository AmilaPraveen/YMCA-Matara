# Sports Programme with Foreign Students

**Date: 2026-01-18**

On January 18, 2026, a sports programme was conducted near Matara beach. The event involved Matara YMCA youth members and youth student groups from Norway, the USA, and Germany.

Around 20 members participated in this programme, which helped to motivate them. The collaborative effort near the beach provided a platform for interaction among the local and international students.
